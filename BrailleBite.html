Braille Bite

NEURAL AIDED AUDITORY SYSTEM FOR AUTONOMOUS FOOD ORDERING BY SIGHTLESS

Objective: This Android application is designed to assist visually impaired individuals in independently ordering food using a voice-guided interface.

Tools Used: Flutter, TTS & STT Api, Firebase

What the user does?

  • Browse restaurant menus
  • Place orders
  • Receive updates on order preparation and delivery

Outcome:The system enhances accessibility, independence, and convenience, resulting in a more inclusive food ordering experience.
